Rush, the Canadian rock band, have enjoyed a remarkable longevity. This means they have tons and tons of lyrics. In fact, if one were to make a Markov matrix of all their lyrics and generate new ones, the results might resemble postmodern poetry or something. This flask web app, if it's still functional, represents my first foray into web programming.
